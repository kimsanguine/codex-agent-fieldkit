# Privacy

This project uses fictional examples and synthetic data.

## Data Rules

- Do not add real customer records.
- Do not add learner or participant identities.
- Do not add screenshots from private workspaces.
- Do not add internal URLs, QR codes, or invite links.
- Do not add exported chat logs unless they are fully redacted and synthetic.

## Safe Example Pattern

Use a fictional organization such as `ACME Life`.

Use synthetic rows with no real person, company, policy, claim, account, email, phone, or address data.

## Handoff Boundary

Handoff packages should describe:

- what exists
- how to run it
- what was verified
- what remains risky

They should not include accounts, credentials, private browser sessions, or hidden workspace access.
